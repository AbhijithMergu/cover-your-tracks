<h5>What is this?</h5>
<p>The dimensions and color depth of your current browser viewport.
</p>
<h5>What kind of information is gathered?</h5>
<p>This can supplement other information when it is not enough to identify your browser, but it is often too ‘brittle’ of a metric to be usable by trackers.  For instance, a user may change their browser dimensions often.
</p>
